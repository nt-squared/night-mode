Members

Gin SK

Gin SK

Gin SK is a Progressive and Electro House main DJ from Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

He used to be a contestant in the Mix Us Happy 2019 DJ contest organized by Pioneer DJ Vietnam.

Emotional, energetic and powerful are the words to describe GinSK's music.

He always aims to transform his sounds into his own unique persona and creates different but irresistible tunes for his audience to enjoy.

GENRE: EDM, Electro/Progressive/House, Tech House

EYED

EYED

Since becoming a DJ, EYED has been used to the hustle and bustle of Saigon city.

To take his dream seriously, EYED constantly learns from seniors after achieving the 1st runner-up prize of Mix Us Happy.

GENRE: Trap, Hiphop, Psy, Tech House, Club House, etc.

K.T

K.T

After participating in Mix Us Happy Vietnam 2019, K.T keeps discovering the magical music world and establishes Night:Mode Collective with his DJ friends.

For him, making people sympathize with others through music is the most wonderful thing.

GENRE: EDM

MOFU

MOFU

MOFU is the youngest music producer/DJ of Night:Mode.

Inspired by Anime and J-Pop, he has created many Bass music tracks from his bed room.

Behind his timid exterior, there's an artist who gives the crowd a lift with crazy performance and positive energy.

GENRE: Bass Music

Auri$

Auri$

From a bedroom DJ sharing her mixset online, Auri$ took the chance to perform at the first Night:Mode : The Listening Party and joined the crew since then.

Joyful and loving tunes are her favorite picks in every performance.

GENRE: Tech House

Tran Phat

Tran Phat

Tran Phat passionates about a variety of sounds and music in different cultures and shares the good vibe with people.

He is the co-founder of Night:Mode, the paperwork guy and also a DJ.

He is part of a formidable duo called LUDENS. Together with MOFU, they share a common vision of the experimentality in Bass Music.

GENRE: Bass Music
